DPIK TUGAS User Guide

How staff sign in, find assigned work, create a Work Thread, and acknowledge an assignment in the current non-production startup.

Start and sign in

  1. On the configured DPIK workstation, double-click Start-TUGAS.cmd.
  2. Wait for the startup health message, then open 127.0.0.1:4173.
  3. Enter your approved work email and choose Send magic link.
  4. Open the newest Supabase sign-in email on the same device and follow its link.

TUGAS does not store a password. Never forward a sign-in link; it represents your session.

Use My Work

After sign-in, TUGAS resolves your active Project membership and shows Work Threads available to your identity.

  • Unassigned: created work that has not yet been delegated.
  • Awaiting acknowledgement: assigned work waiting for the assignee to confirm receipt.
  • Assigned: the assignee has acknowledged the instruction.

Create, assign, and acknowledge

Create

Authorized Work Owners enter a clear title, expected outcome, and source reference, then choose Create.

Assign

In the startup fixture, the Work Owner can assign an unassigned item to Smoque with the displayed due commitment and reason.

Acknowledge

The named assignee signs in, opens My Work, and chooses Acknowledge. Do not acknowledge on another person's behalf.

If something goes wrong

  • No email: check spam, confirm the address, wait briefly, then request one new link.
  • No Project membership: contact the DPIK TUGAS administrator; do not use another person's account.
  • Stale or rejected action: refresh My Work before retrying. Report repeated errors with the time and visible message.
  • Startup unavailable: close the startup window, run Start-TUGAS.cmd once, and report the log location shown.

This is a non-production reference slice. InProgress, Blocked, acceptance, closure, and production use are not yet authorized.
