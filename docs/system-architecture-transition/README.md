## Product boundary

TUGAS owns accountability, guarded work progression, Deliverable Revision history, review decisions, Submission manifests, Dispatch custody attempts, Receipt Evidence attempts and decisions, Claim Requirement readiness, management attention and business audit history.

Gmail, Outlook, WhatsApp and meetings remain communication sources. Word, Excel and CAD remain authoring tools. Drive and SharePoint remain authoritative document repositories. TUGAS does not become a claim-valuation or courier-routing engine.
